\subsection*{Research and Professional Experience}
Professeur Invite, Institute de Physique du Globe de Paris, 03/2013--05/2013. \emph{Description}: Research into the role chaos plays in the reversal statistics of Earth's magnetic field by combing simplified ODE models with advanced Monte Carlo sampling.
